## The Challenge

Justin’s educational organization was struggling with manual lesson plan creation, consuming over 25 hours per week and causing inconsistent educational quality.

### Pain Points:

- 25+ hours weekly lost to manual lesson planning
- Quality variance across instructors
- 2–3 hours spent per lesson on resource discovery
- No standardized assessments
- Limited scalability with a growing student base

---

## The Solution

RapidXAI built EduForge AI, an AI-powered curriculum management engine automating lesson creation, assessments, and student progress tracking.

### Core Features:

- **AI Lesson Generator:** Converts topics into detailed lesson plans in under 2 minutes
- **Smart Resource Finder:** Instantly discovers curated educational materials
- **Assessment Creator:** Auto-generates quizzes and evaluation sheets
- **Student Tracking:** Provides real-time learning analytics
- **Curriculum Mapping:** Ensures standards-aligned progress
- **Multi-Subject Support:** Enables cross-curricular integrations
