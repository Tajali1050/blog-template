Case Study 1 — DialEstatePro.xyz: Real Estate Cold Calling Revolution
Client: Jahmai Nicome — Real Estate Investment Professional
Industry: Real Estate Investment
Project Value: $1,000
Platform: dialestatepro.xyz

Demo Video: Loom Recording
The Challenge
Jahmai needed to systematically contact 250 brokers twice weekly for investment opportunities. The
manual process consumed 6–8 hours daily, limiting scalability and causing inconsistent follow-up
procedures.

Pain Points:
- Manual dialing consuming entire workdays
- Inconsistent call quality and messaging
- Missed opportunities due to time constraints
- No systematic follow-up tracking
- Compliance management complexity

The Solution:
RapidXAI developed DialEstatePro.xyz, a comprehensive cold-calling automation platform designed for
real estate professionals.

Key Features Implemented:
- Power Dialing System: Intelligent number management with predictive algorithms
- Spreadsheet Integration: Bulk upload and contact management
- Call Distribution: Automated scheduling during business hours
- Compliance Tools: DNC list management and regulatory adherence
- Performance Analytics: Real-time tracking and optimization
- AI Voice Integration: High-quality, human-like voice agents

Measurable Results:
“The voice agent system calls 250 brokers twice weekly with superior quality to our previous
solution. Cost per connected minute dropped to 7¢ while maintaining high conversion rates.
This is game-changing automation for my business.”
— Jahmai Nicome, Real Estate Investor
